Who we are

We're a tight-knit team of blockchain engineers, designers, and operators based in Berlin. Since 2020, we've been building decentralized infrastructure for organizations like the Ethereum Foundation, MakerDAO, and Protocol Labs.

We don't just write code — we ship products, run nodes, and keep systems alive.

What we do

We help Web3 projects go from idea to production — and stay there. Whether you need a smart contract audit, a full-stack dApp, or someone to run your infrastructure, we've got you covered.

Our services: Consulting, Development, and Operations.

What we stand for

Open source. Decentralization. Shipping over talking.

We believe the future is permissionless — and we're building it. Every project we take on moves the ecosystem forward.
